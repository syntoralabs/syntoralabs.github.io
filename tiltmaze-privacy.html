Privacy Policy for Tilt Maze

Effective date: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tilt Maze, published by Syntora Labs, handles information when you use the mobile game.

Information We Collect

Tilt Maze does not ask you to create an account and does not directly collect your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, or messages.

The app may process the following information:

  • Device motion sensor data, used to control the ball during gameplay.
  • Gameplay data stored locally on your device, such as level progress, coins, stars, settings, selected theme, calibration, sound, vibration, and rolling speed.
  • Advertising data handled by Google AdMob to load, show, measure, and improve ads.

How We Use Information

  • To run the game and let you control the ball using device motion.
  • To save your progress and settings on your device.
  • To provide vibration and sound feedback when enabled.
  • To show banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads through Google AdMob.
  • To maintain and improve the app experience.

Advertising

Tilt Maze uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service. AdMob may collect or receive information from your device, including device identifiers, ad interaction data, approximate location derived from IP address, diagnostics, and other information used to serve and measure ads.

Google and its partners may use this information to show personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on your settings, consent choices, and applicable law.

Learn more about how Google uses information for ads: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Learn more about Google partners: https://policies.google.com/privacy/google-partners

Device Motion and Vibration

Tilt Maze uses device motion sensor data to control gameplay. This data is used in real time inside the app and is not stored on our servers by Tilt Maze. The app may also use vibration for gameplay feedback if you keep vibration enabled.

Local Storage

Tilt Maze stores gameplay progress and preferences locally on your device using app storage. This includes items such as unlocked levels, coins, stars, calibration, selected maze theme, sound setting, vibration setting, and rolling speed. You can remove this local data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage through your device settings.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by third-party service providers such as Google AdMob as described above. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the security of the app.

Children's Privacy

Tilt Maze is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Choices

  • You can change sound, vibration, calibration, theme, and rolling speed settings inside the app.
  • You can reset local app data by clearing storage or uninstalling the app.
  • You can manage ad personalization through your device settings and Google ad settings.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information associated with the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
