Privacy Policy

App: TickDay
Developer: Syntora Labs
Effective date: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TickDay (“the App”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App. If you have questions, contact us at support.syntoralabs@gmail.com.

Summary

  • The App is designed to work without creating an account.
  • Your habit/ritual data is stored locally on your device (unless you choose to share it outside the App).
  • The App shows ads using Google Mobile Ads (AdMob), which may collect device identifiers and usage data to provide and measure ads.
  • The App uses notifications/reminders, including after device restart.

Information the App Collects

1) Information you provide

  • Rituals/habits and settings: content you enter (e.g., routines, reminder times, preferences). Stored locally on your device.
  • Images (optional): if you choose to pick an image (e.g., for personalization), the App accesses the image you select. The App does not upload your images to our servers.

2) Automatically collected information

The App itself does not operate its own backend servers for accounts. However, third-party services used by the App (listed below) may collect certain information automatically, such as:

  • Device information (model, OS version, language/region)
  • App interaction and basic diagnostics
  • Identifiers used for advertising (such as the Advertising ID), where available
  • IP address (typically used for network communication and ad delivery)

How Information Is Used

  • To provide core app functionality (habits, routines, reminders, and preferences)
  • To deliver notifications you configure
  • To display and measure ads (if ads are shown)
  • To maintain app stability and prevent abuse/fraud related to ad delivery

Third-Party Services (Ads)

TickDay uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display ads. AdMob may collect and process information to provide, personalize (where permitted), and measure ads, and to detect fraud/abuse.

For more details, review Google’s privacy information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with third-party providers strictly to operate the App’s ad functionality (e.g., Google/AdMob as described above).

Permissions

  • Notifications: used to send reminders you set.
  • Receive Boot Completed: used to re-schedule reminders after device restart.
  • Photos/Media (optional): used only when you choose to pick an image.
  • Internet: required for loading ads and related services.

Data Storage, Retention, and Deletion

  • Your app content (habits/rituals) is stored locally on your device.
  • You can delete your data by clearing the App’s storage or uninstalling the App.
  • Third-party services (e.g., AdMob) may retain data according to their own policies.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information within the App, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page and update the effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: support.syntoralabs@gmail.com
